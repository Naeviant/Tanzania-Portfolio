Fundraising

Fundraising the £3,890 required for the expedition was by no means easy. Below is a summary of some of the key points in my fundraising. It is very important to note that there were other additional donations from several different people - all of which I am very grateful for.

  • Bag Packing - M&S

    On the mornings of the 21st, 22nd and 23rd of December 2017, I took part in bag packing at M&S in Ashton-under-Lyne.

    This raised a total of £114.84.

  • Raffle - Manchester City Family Tour Voucher

    In resposne to a collection of letters I sent out to several local organisations, Manchester City Football Club very kindly donated a Family Tour Voucher to me. I raffled this off to all form groups and staff at West Hill School, and later drew the winner: a year 7 student.

    This raised a total of £39.60.

  • Selling Refreshments - West Hill School

    On the 22nd March 2018, myself and some of the other fundraisers arranged to sell refreshments during the Year 8 Parents' Evening in the school canteen.

    This raised a total of £23.00.

  • Car Boot Sale - West Hill School

    On the 1st June 2018, a car boot sale was held at West Hill School which I took part in - selling various items I no longer needed.

    This raised a total of £52.50.

  • Sponsorship - Harrison Catering

    In resposne to a collection of letters I sent out to several local organisations, Harrison Catering - who have a partnership with West Hill School - very kindly donated a cheque to contribute towards my fundraising. Additionally, they also donated an iPad to raffle off in order to raise funds for the wider group.

  • Bag Packing - Morrisons

    On the afternoon of the 9th June 2018, I took part in bag packing at Morrisons in Dukinfield.

    This raised a total of £39.79.

  • Employment

    As of the 2nd September 2018, I have been working in a retail job which has allowed me to pay off the remaining balance and also buy the kit that I required.
